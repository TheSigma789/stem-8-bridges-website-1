#Test 2

Design process:

When designing we did a lot of similar things to test 1, we drew a design on a paper and used it to create our spaghetti members. This time we made some of our mebers thicker to suport more weight and we made a camelback truss.

Testing and results:

We had a mass of 174g and carried 10kg which gave us 57.5 points landing us in third again.

Reflections and improvements

Our failure mode was compression on the most right upper beam, something to take note of is it was the longest upper beam. Were going to make those thicker and remove thickness for members that dont hold much force.
